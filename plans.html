Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden fees or setup charges?+
Absolutely not. The monthly price you see is all you pay. There are no setup charges, no equipment rental fees, no annual price hikes, and no data overage fees. AMACNO believes in 100% transparent pricing.
How long does installation take?+
Most installations are completed within 48–72 hours of sign-up. Our technicians typically complete the full setup — including router configuration and device testing — in 2–3 hours on-site.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. You can change your plan at any time with no penalty fees. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply from the next billing cycle. Just contact our support team.
Is there a contract or lock-in period?+
Our home plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract required. Business plans may have optional 12 or 24-month agreements that offer additional discounts.
What areas does AMACNO service?+
We currently operate across 20+ cities in India including Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, and more. Contact us to check availability in your specific area or township.
What is AMACNO's role as a service provider?+
AMACNO PVT LTD is an authorized dealer and service facilitator. We work with leading telecom operators to connect you to their networks. Our service fees cover installation coordination, account management, plan changes, and technical support — separate from your provider's monthly service charges.

Not Sure Which Plan Is Right for You?

Our experts are happy to help you choose the best plan based on your usage, household size, and budget — at no cost.
