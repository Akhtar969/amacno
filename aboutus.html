Our Story

15 Years of Connecting Communities

Founded in 2010 in Mumbai, AMACNO PVT LTD began as a small team of telecom professionals frustrated by the lack of honest, customer-first internet services in India. We set out to change that — starting with a single promise: no hidden fees, ever.

Over 15 years, we've grown into one of India's most trusted connectivity facilitators, serving over 50,000 customers across major cities and townships. Our model is simple: partner with the best providers, charge fair prices, and deliver exceptional service at every touchpoint.

Today, AMACNO operates as an authorized dealer for India's leading telecom and cable operators, giving our customers access to the widest range of services — all managed through a single, dedicated support team available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
